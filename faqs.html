Project Submission

Project submission will be taking place on our newly written platform, Spectacle!
The link for Spectacle will appear here and on the front page of dayof when the time comes. Please read the following instructions carefully!

Submission Instructions

  1. Go to Spectacle and login with your my.hackmit.org account!
  2. From there, make a team or join one from "Manage Team" to get started!
  3. Hit "Submit Project" when you're ready, and use "Edit Project" from "Manage Team" afterwards to make any changes before the deadline.
  4. You will be asked to provide a virtual room link (i.e. zoom) that judges can access. Please make sure this link is submitted correctly on Spectacle or judges may be unable to find you!

Judging

Judging this year will take place on our new platform, Ballot!
Our live judging will be schedule based. A master schedule will be generated once project submission completes. You will be sent a judging schedule over email 15 minutes before judging begins. Here's the rundown:

  • The two hours of judging will be broken down into 10 minute time slots
  • Sponsor judging and expo judging will take place simultaneously
  • You will have 4 minutes to present your project and another 4 minutes maximum for judge's Q/A. Judges will be instructed to cut you off after 8 minutes.
  • You will be scheduled for 1 slot for expo judges, and as many slots as we can fit for the rest of your sponsor challenges
  • It is very important that you are on your video room during your alloted times. However, when you are not scheduled you are not obligated to stay on video call.
