Hack Playground is a virtual world that brings HackMIT, one of the largest undergraduate hackathons, right to your home. Playground preserves the atmosphere of innovation and connection of a hackathon. You can walk through the Sponsor Town and schedule a coffee chat with a sponsor, gain inspiration by visiting the Nonprofit Village, or chat with other hackers. Not only that, Playground is also a place to have fun, and we hope you’ll have fun trying to earn all the achievements or show off your cool dance moves.

Highlighted features:
  • Take a walk through the Sponsor Town and learn more about our sponsors. You can also join the queue to talk to sponsors or set up a coffee chat.
  • We’ve partnered with nonprofits this year, and we hope you’ll gain some inspiration from them after exploring our Nonprofit Village.
  • Are you an MIT student interested in the free international program that MISTI has to offer? Come chat with MISTI representatives and learn about the MIT exclusive international opportunities!
    Sat 10:30-11 AM EDT, 4:30-5:00 PM EDT

Just for fun:
  • Chat with hackers around you! Feel free to change your bio.
  • Dance in the Hack Club ;), located in the basement of the shopping mall. (only if you’re a college student!)
  • See where other hackers are from on the hacker map, located in the coffee shop.
  • Earn all the achievements on Playground Explorer to earn raffle tickets for special prizes.
  • And more! We encourage you to explore : D
