Travel

HackMIT Bus

Arriving

You should have been contacted by your bus point already about when and where the bus will be picking you up from your school.

All buses will arrive at 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA 02139

Departing

Buses for the following cities leave Sunday afternoon at 4pm from 77 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge, MA 02139

  • Princeton, NJ
  • New York, NY
  • Providence, RI
  • Hartford, CT

Plane

Arriving

Take the SILVER LINE towards South Station.

Once at South Station, take the RED LINE toward Alewife and get off at the Kendall/MIT stop.

To get to Kresge Auditorium, follow the map on the maps page.

Departing

Walk to the T stop in Kendall Square and take the RED LINE towards Ashmont/ Braintree.

Get off at South Station and take the SILVER LINE to Logan International Airport.
