I’m tired! How do I go to sleep?
Call your host, ask them to pick you up, and they'll escort you to their not-at-all cramped rooms.
Can I leave Johnson whenever I want?
For security reasons, if you want to leave Johnson you'll need to call your host and have him/her escort you back.
I need technical help. How do I get help on [API, language]?
We have a help queue and mentors to help you along!
I’m hungry! How do I get food?
We’ll have food events throughout the day/night, check the day-of booklet or this website for information. We also have an abundant supply of healthy and non-healthy snacks at the concession stand in Johnson hallway.
Something isn’t working! I’m confused! What even is doge???
Please come to the Helpdesk and tell us--we’ll be happy to help!
When do I need to submit my project by?
Judging will begin promptly at 11am Sunday, so you'll need to submit your project by then (only accessible Sunday!).
When do I need to form my team by?
We will have a team formation event from 11:00am-12:00pm on Saturday. Let us know who's on your team and where you're all working as soon as you can. You'll need to tell us your team and location (table number) by 9:30am Sunday.
How does judging work?
We will have a judging expo on Sunday from 11am-12:30pm. Feel free to wander around and check out other projects, but make sure at least one of your team members is at your table to present to judges. The top teams will move on to an additional round of panel judging.
