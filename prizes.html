Project submissions must be in by 11:15 AM on Sunday and should be submitted through DevPost. To submit your project:

  • Register for event at go.hackmit.org/devpost
  • Click 'Get started!' and add your project

All projects must be submitted to one of the tracks listed. Projects can additionally be submitted to optional challenges for a chance to win other prizes! At the expo, each team will present their projects for a few minutes at a time to small groups of judges in a science-fair format. No need to make a fancy presentation -- the judges will be visiting a lot of tables, so you'll only be giving quick, informal pitches as they walk around the hacking arena! After expo judging ends at 1:00 PM, the top teams will be chosen for sponsor judging.
